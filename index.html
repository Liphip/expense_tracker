Expense Tracker

Welcome to Expense Tracker

Expense Tracker is a comprehensive web application designed to help you manage and track your expenses efficiently. It is ideal for group expenses, such as trips, events, and shared activities. With features like adding expenses, guests, and tags, and providing detailed overviews and downloadable reports, it simplifies expense management.

Expense Tracker is open source and available on GitHub.

How to Use Expense Tracker

  • Add Expense: Enter the name of the expense and the amount, then click the add button. The expense will be added to the table, and the total and average will be updated.
  • Delete Expense: Click the delete button to remove an expense from the table. The total and average will be updated accordingly.
  • Clear All Expenses: Click the clear button to remove all expenses from the table. The total and average will be reset.
  • Download CSV: Click the download button to download a CSV file of the expenses. The CSV file can be opened in spreadsheet programs such as Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.
  • Save and Load Data: Use the options to save your data to a file or load data from a file, ensuring your expense records are always up-to-date and accessible.
  • Add Guests and Tags: Add guests and tags to categorize your expenses and get detailed insights into your spending patterns.
  • View Overview: View the total expenses, average expenses per guest, and average expenses per tag in the overview section.
  • Manage Guests and Tags: Manage your guests and tags efficiently by adding, clearing, and associating them with expenses.

Features of Expense Tracker

  • Easy expense tracking for groups, trips, events, and shared activities.
  • Add and manage guests and tags for better categorization.
  • Detailed overview of total expenses, average expenses per guest, and average expenses per tag.
  • Downloadable reports in CSV format for easy sharing and analysis.
  • Save and load expense data to and from files for persistent record-keeping.
  • Clear and intuitive user interface for seamless expense management.
  • Open source and available on GitHub for community contributions and improvements.
